Jesse Michael Han (Feb 08 2021 at 21:40):

New feature: the autoname tactic will ask GPT-f to suggest a name for the main goal. You can use it by importing tactic.autoname. It seems to suggest sensible names for even nonexistent lemmas. Please try it out and let us know if it makes interesting suggestions!

Please note, autonameonly works with the current model formal-lean-wm-to-tt-m1-m2-v4-c4.

Jesse Michael Han (Feb 09 2021 at 04:31):

It seems to suggest sensible names for even nonexistent lemmas.

On a completely unseen test set of ~2800 theorems from future (relative to the training data) commits of mathlib, it correctly guesses the names of 20% of them on the first try, 27% within 10 tries, and 30% within 16 tries.
