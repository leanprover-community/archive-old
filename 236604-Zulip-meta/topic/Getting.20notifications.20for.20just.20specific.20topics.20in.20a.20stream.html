Utensil Song (Jul 27 2020 at 01:52):

This tip might be related in the world without notifications for topics:

Every-time you wish to check the updates in the topics you participated, open "Recent topics" (at the top left corner of the Zulip client) and choose "Participated".

(This is a beta feature, you might need to update the client)

Utensil Song (Jul 27 2020 at 01:54):

Before there's this feature, I've been using another trick:

Click the vertical 3 dots on the right of my used id in "USERS" (on the left of the Zulip client), and choose "View messages sent".

(I've been using this trick to track @Eric Wieser too)
