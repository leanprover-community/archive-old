Julian Berman (Mar 26 2021 at 13:59):

When GitHub sends emails, they tag an email header which says the reason the email was sent to you

Julian Berman (Mar 26 2021 at 14:00):

So if it's helpful to you by chance, you can differentiate between emails sent to you because you're subscribed to a repo and emails sent because you're mentioned, say.

Julian Berman (Mar 26 2021 at 14:03):

https://docs.github.com/en/github/managing-subscriptions-and-notifications-on-github/configuring-notifications#filtering-email-notifications -- looks like these days it's via the CC header (which gets set to a different email address depending on why you got the notification, so for mentions, it's mention@noreply.github.com )
