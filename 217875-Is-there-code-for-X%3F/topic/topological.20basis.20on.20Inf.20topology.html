Adam Topaz (May 26 2021 at 19:26):

Given a collection of topological spaces XiX_iXi​ and maps fi:X→Xif_i : X \to X_ifi​:X→Xi​ from a fixed XXX, we can form the coarsest topology on XXX for which the fif_ifi​ are continuous. We can formalize this in lean as the Inf of the induced topologies along the fif_ifi​ as iii varies. Do we have a description of a topological basis for this topology on XXX?

Patrick Massot (May 26 2021 at 19:31):

We have very few results describing bases for topologies, because they are much less useful than filter bases. Here we definitely have results about the neighborhoods for this topology. What are you trying to do?

The cofiltered assertion is crucial for this, but for now I'm just trying to get a handle of working with the (cl)opens in XXX.
