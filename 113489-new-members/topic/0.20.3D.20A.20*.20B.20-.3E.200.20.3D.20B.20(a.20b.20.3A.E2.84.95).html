Rick Love (Aug 17 2020 at 03:30):

Ok, figured out how to use that with cases:

theorem equation_zero_cancel_mul (A B :ℕ) (ha: A > 0):
    0 = A * B -> 0 = B :=
begin
    intros h,
    cases eq_zero_of_mul_eq_zero h.symm,
    linarith,
    exact h_1.symm,
end
