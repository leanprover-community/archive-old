Kevin Buzzard (Jul 02 2021 at 07:50):

If you're a mathematician then I would put off learning type theory. I taught a Lean course for mathematicians https://github.com/ImperialCollegeLondon/formalising-mathematics and I just told them "when Lean says G : Type just read it as "let GGG be a set", and when it says g : G that means "let ggg be an element of GGG".
