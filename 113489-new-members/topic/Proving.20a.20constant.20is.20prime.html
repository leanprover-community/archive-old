Newell Jensen (Aug 24 2022 at 20:29):

Here should be an mwe:

import data.nat.prime

namespace nat

example : prime 5 := by norm_num

example (p : ℕ): prime p :=
begin
  let p₀ := 5,
  have h : prime p₀ := by { unfold p₀, norm_num }
end

Thanks for all the replies, I truly appreciate it.
