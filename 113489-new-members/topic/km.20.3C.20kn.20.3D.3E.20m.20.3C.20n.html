Kevin Buzzard (Jun 03 2020 at 16:35):

lemma lt_mul_cancel (n m k : ℕ) (h_k : 1 < k) : (k*m + 1 < k*n) → (m < n) :=
begin
  intro h,
  refine lt_of_mul_lt_mul_left _ (show 0 ≤ k, by linarith),
    refine lt_trans _ h,
    omega,
end

There's a proof which works over nat
