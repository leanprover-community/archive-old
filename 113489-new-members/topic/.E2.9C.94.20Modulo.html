Since I cannot delete my (meaningless) topic, I will leave here an answer to an improved version of my own question above.

lemma unmodulo (a m : ℕ) (m_pos : m > 0) : (a % m) = a - m * (a / m) :=
eq_sub_of_add_eq'' (nat.mod_add_div a m)
