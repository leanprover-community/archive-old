I have the following goal state:

1 goal
fg: ℝ → ℝ
ab: ℝ
hfa: fn_lb f a
hgb: fn_lb g b
⊢ fn_lb (λ (x : ℝ), f x + g x) (a + b)

Is there a tactic for unfolding the definition offn_lb in this goal state?

Jalex Stark (Jul 22 2020 at 04:16):
