Heather Macbeth (Sep 01 2020 at 16:52):

What is the recommended way to do problems that amount to "ring", but with a few certified-nonzero objects and their products in denominators? Surely there is something better than this:

example (x : ℝ) (h : 7 * x - 1 ≠ 0): x / (7 * x - 1) - 1 / 7 = 1 / (7 * (7 * x - 1)) :=
begin
  refine (mul_left_inj' h).mp _,
  rw div_mul_eq_div_mul_one_div,
  rw mul_assoc,
  rw div_mul_cancel _ h,
  field_simp [h],
  cancel_denoms,
  ring,
end

Sebastien Gouezel (Sep 01 2020 at 16:59):

example (x : ℝ) (h : 7 * x - 1 ≠ 0): x / (7 * x - 1) - 1 / 7 = 1 / (7 * (7 * x - 1)) :=
begin
  have : (7 : ℝ) ≠ 0, by norm_num,
  field_simp [h, this],
  ring,
end
