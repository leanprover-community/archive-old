Reid Barton (Sep 08 2020 at 14:43):

def uncurry (f : α → β → γ) : α × β → γ := λ ⟨a, b⟩, f a b

(a, b) isn't supported as the argument of a lambda. Therefore you also have to tell Lean the expected type of the function.
