Martin Dvořák (May 23 2022 at 19:34):

In case somebody is interested in this little utility:

namespace prod

variables {α β γ : Type}

@[simp] def first (t : α × β × γ) : α := t.fst
@[simp] def secon (t : α × β × γ) : β := t.snd.fst
@[simp] def third (t : α × β × γ) : γ := t.snd.snd

def firstwo (t : α × β × γ) : α × β := (t.first, t.secon)

end prod
