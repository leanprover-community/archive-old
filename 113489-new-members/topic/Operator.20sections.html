Does lean have any equivalent of Haskell's operator sections?

Edward Ayers (Nov 13 2019 at 15:50):

You can do

#check (× nat) int -- int × nat
#check (+ 3) 2
#check ((×) nat) int -- nat × int
#check ((+) 3) 2

But (3 +) is a parse error.
