Alex Zhang (Jun 30 2021 at 05:33):

What does it mean when #eval something returns "result type does not have an instance of type class 'has_repr', dumping internal representation".

Johan Commelin (Jun 30 2021 at 05:35):

It means that lean doesn't know a nice way of printing the result

Johan Commelin (Jun 30 2021 at 05:35):

You can create an instance of has_repr my_fancy_type and tell lean how to convert terms of type my_fancy_type into strings. It will then use that when printing the result.
