Alex J. Best (Nov 30 2020 at 03:07):

The most tactical proof I can find is

example (d : ℕ) : 2 * (2 ^ d - 1) + 1 = 2 ^ d.succ - 1 :=
begin
  zify [nat.one_le_pow'],
  change d.succ with d + 1,
  ring_exp,
end
