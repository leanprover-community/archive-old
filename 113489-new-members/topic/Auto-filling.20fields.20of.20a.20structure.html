{
to_fun    := sorry,
inv_fun   := sorry,
left_inv  := sorry,
right_inv := sorry,
}

Johan Commelin (Jan 12 2022 at 17:08):

write _, and click on the :bulb: then select the item containing "skeleton"

Johan Commelin (Jan 12 2022 at 17:08):

I think Ctrl+. is a shortcut for the :bulb:

Johan Commelin (Jan 12 2022 at 17:08):

def my_equiv : α ≃ β :=
_ -- put your cursor on the `_`
