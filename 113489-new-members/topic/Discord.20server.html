Damiano Testa (Jul 03 2021 at 15:12):

I also want to emphasize one of the great strengths of formalizing. If you formalize a proof, thinking of your special case (e.g. Q\mathbb{Q}Q in this case), and Lean confirms that all the steps work for a general global field, you achieve two goals:

  • you proved a more general result;
  • normally you also understand the proof in the more general case much better!

This is a very important consequence of using a proof assistant!
