Reid Barton (Oct 28 2019 at 15:15):

It's easier with the nesting structure displayed

| ¬ (p ∨ ¬ p)
| | p
| | p ∨ ¬ p
| | ⊥
| ¬ p
| p ∨ ¬ p
| ⊥
¬ ¬ (p ∨ ¬ p)
p ∨ ¬ p
