As far as I understand, the three exact after the refine fill in the underscores. Thus, also refine nontrivial_of_ne set.univ âˆ… set.empty_ne_univ.symm, finishes the goal. At this point, you can notice that the last underscore is filled in with a proof that âˆ… â‰  set.univ, so just this is enough for Lean to figure out what the two sets in question were. Hence, refine nontrivial_of_ne _ _ set.empty_ne_univ.symm,is sufficient information to create a proof. Once you are here, you no longer need tactic mode:
