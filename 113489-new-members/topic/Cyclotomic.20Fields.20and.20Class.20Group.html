Dev-Indra (Apr 14 2020 at 19:16):

This is unrelated to Zulip, but does anyone know a book, article, pdf, that proves why the ideal class group of cyclotomic integers in finite? and whether prime ideals in this ring have prime norm?

Kevin Buzzard (Apr 14 2020 at 19:18):

The ideal class group of any global field is finite, and for any number field other than the rationals there are prime ideals with non-prime norm.

Dev-Indra (Apr 14 2020 at 19:20):

Why is the first claim true? (I know its true for quadratic number fields only)

Kevin Buzzard (Apr 14 2020 at 19:22):

This is a standard fact proved in any book on algebraic number theory at this level e.g. Marcus "Number fields".
