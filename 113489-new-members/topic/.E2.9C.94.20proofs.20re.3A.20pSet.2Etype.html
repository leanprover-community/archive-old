Yaël Dillies (Jun 15 2022 at 23:52):

import set_theory.zfc

lemma pSet_powerset_type (s : pSet.{0}) : (pSet.powerset s).type = set s.type :=
by { induction s, refl }

Yaël Dillies (Jun 15 2022 at 23:53):

docs#pSet.powerset is defined by induction, so you need induction to prove stuff about it.
