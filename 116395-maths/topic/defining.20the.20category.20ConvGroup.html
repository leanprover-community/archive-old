Patrick Massot (Jan 02 2022 at 16:17):

I would say this ‚¶É ‚¶Ñ trick is an accidental workaround. This is an elaborator bug, your code should work (and you can make it work it you use @ to help. Your definition of continuity is known to bring elaboration issues. That's why continuous is a structure in mathlib. You can use the following code:
