Kenny Lau (Aug 10 2018 at 22:10):

run_cmd tactic.skip

#find ¬ _ ≤ _ ↔ _ < _
-- not_le: ∀ {α : Type u} [_inst_1 : linear_order α] {a b : α}, ¬a ≤ b ↔ b < a
