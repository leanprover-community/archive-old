Why is docs#category_theory.subobject.map a functor instead of a regular function between lattices? @Bhavik Mehta @Scott Morrison

Bhavik Mehta (Sep 24 2021 at 15:30):

I believe there are legitimate use cases for both - iirc there's a function to convert from the functor to a (monotone) function. It's nice to define it this way because the functorial properties for most of the functions on lattices can be deduced immediately from functors in the over category, and there are times you want to compose the functor with other functors which aren't just on lattices
