μ0\mu_0μ0​ and λ\lambdaλ will be cardinals - and yes, it's about model-theoretic stability. An example: a lot of the time we are concerned about building trees level by level, and we violate stability at λ\lambdaλ by building a tree of height μ0\mu_0μ0​. The question is then if we have a tree of height μ0\mu_0μ0​ (violating stability), can we extend this tree to a tree of height λ\lambdaλ. Part of the motivation here is that under GCH, μ0=λ\mu_0 = \lambdaμ0​=λ.

I hope the question is clear now :)
