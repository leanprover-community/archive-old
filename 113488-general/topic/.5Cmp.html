Chris Hughes (Jul 14 2020 at 11:35):

Sometimes it can be convenient to prove the theorem using an element of units ℤ. With ε : units ℤ, you could say
sinm (x + ε * pi) m = - ε * sinm x m. I have used this in the past to write a prof, but it probably doesn't make this example any easier, and you usually will want both statements, even if the ε trick makes the proof easier.
