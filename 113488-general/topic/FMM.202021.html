Mario Carneiro (Jul 30 2021 at 02:26):

Just a heads up for folks who want to catch it live: CICM 2021 is going on this week, and the FMM (formal methods for mathematics) workshop on Friday and Saturday contains several talks from Lean folks (agenda):

  • 2021-07-30T13:30:00+02:00: Mario Carneiro - Porting Mathlib (invited talk) (abstract)
  • 2021-07-30T17:00:00+02:00: Muhammad Harun Ali Khan - Formalizing Fibonacci Squares (abstract)
  • 2021-07-30T18:00:00+02:00: Alexander Bentkamp and Jeremy Avigad - Verified Optimization (work in progress) (abstract)
  • 2021-07-31T15:00:00+02:00: Sebastien Gouezel - Formalizing the Gromov-Hausdorff space (abstract)
  • 2021-07-31T15:30:00+02:00: Anthony Bordg and Nicolo Cavalleri - Elements of Differential Geometry in Lean: A Report for Mathematicians (abstract)
  • 2021-07-31T14:00:00+02:00: Kensho Tsurusaki and Akiko Aizawa - Assimilating the structure of formal and informal proof (abstract)
  • 2021-07-31T15:00:00+02:00: Jeremy Avigad - The design of mathematical language (invited talk)
  • 2021-07-31T16:30:00+02:00: Yury Kudryashov - Formalizing rotation number and its properties in Lean (abstract)
  • 2021-07-31T17:30:00+02:00: Eric Wieser - Scalar actions in Lean's mathlib (abstract)
  • 2021-07-31T18:00:00+02:00: Alexander Best - Automatically Generalizing Theorems Using Typeclasses (abstract)
