Eric Wieser (Apr 30 2022 at 20:33):

Do we want to use unicode for ¬∃ like we do for ∉?

-- `¬` on the right to make nested binders behave
notation `∄` binders `, ` r:(scoped P, Exists P) := ¬r

-- notation means the obvious thing
example : (∄ a b c : ℕ, a^2 + b^2 = c^2) ↔ ¬∃ a b c : ℕ, a^2 + b^2 = c^2 :=
iff.rfl
