The first one is a structure, the second one is an inductive

Mario Carneiro (Jul 01 2021 at 17:21):

structure House :=
(color : Color)
(man : Man)
(pet : Pet)
(drink : Drink)
(smoke : Smoke)

inductive Color
| Red | Green | Blue | Yellow | White
