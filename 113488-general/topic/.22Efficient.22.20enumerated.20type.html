Jannis Limperg (Jan 25 2022 at 16:53):

Hacky workaround:

inductive Data
| data1 : Data1
| data2 : Data2

inductive Data1
| foo1
| ...
| foo500

inductive Data2
| foo501
...
| foo1000

Repeat for quadratic decrease in the number of constructors (and logarithmic increase in programmer annoyance).
