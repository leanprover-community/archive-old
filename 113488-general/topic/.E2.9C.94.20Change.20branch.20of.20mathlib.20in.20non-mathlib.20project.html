Alex J. Best (Nov 15 2022 at 06:25):

You can edit the leanpkg.toml file by hand to specify a commit, just change the rev parameter to the commit you want in the lines:

[dependencies]
mathlib = {git = "https://github.com/leanprover-community/mathlib", rev = "8c812fd49c82a071648641af2f12a79a720a4254"}
