Chris B (Dec 31 2021 at 08:01):

This is a US perspective, but if you're doing the "separate copyright headers per file" thing, what matters is the date of publication, which is whenever it was made available to the public.

This is incorrect. Again, this is a US perspective, but you can find info about copyright notice for collective works in 17 U.S.C. § 401. Notice isn't required for a work to be protected, but it's far from irrelevant.
