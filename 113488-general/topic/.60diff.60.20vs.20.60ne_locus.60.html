Damiano Testa (Aug 17 2022 at 15:42):

Dear All,

in #16091, I am introducing the following definition:

import data.finsupp.basic

namespace finsupp
variables {α N : Type*} [decidable_eq α] [decidable_eq N] [has_zero N]

def to_be_named (f g : α →₀ N) : finset α :=
(f.support ∪ g.support).filter (λ x, f x ≠ g x)

end finsupp

Thus, finsupp.to_be_named f g : finset α is the finite subset of α where f and g differ. For instance, in the case in which N is an additive group, finsupp.to_be_named f g coincides with finsupp.support (f - g).

I chose diff as the name.
@Eric Wieser suggested ne_locus as an alternative name. I like both and am slightly in favour of diff just because it is shorter.

Which name do you prefer?
