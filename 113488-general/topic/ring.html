import tactic.ring

Mario Carneiro (Nov 16 2019 at 10:28):

In any case, you can solve the lemma like this:

lemma come_on_ring (x y : ℂ) :
   x * (2 : ℂ)⁻¹ = -((-1) * (2 : ℂ)⁻¹ * x) :=
by {generalize: (2 : ℂ)⁻¹ = a, ring}
