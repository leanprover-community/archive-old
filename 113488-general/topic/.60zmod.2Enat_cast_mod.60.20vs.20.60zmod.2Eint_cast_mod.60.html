Michael Stoll (May 05 2022 at 18:20):

We have

 theorem zmod.nat_cast_mod (n a : ℕ) : ↑(a % n) = ↑a
 theorem zmod.int_cast_mod (a : ℤ) (b : ℕ) : ↑(a % ↑b) = ↑a

The order of arguments is not compatible.
