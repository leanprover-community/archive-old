  • Who should we credit as authors?

Eric Wieser (Sep 08 2021 at 17:36):

Sometimes the git blame shows the entire contents of the split was added in a single commit - if so, then using the authors for that commit is probably reasonable

Eric Wieser (Sep 08 2021 at 17:37):

Mario Carneiro (Sep 08 2021 at 17:59):

Mario Carneiro (Sep 08 2021 at 18:01):
