Mac (Oct 18 2022 at 18:08):

The Std.Tactic folder is a holdover from mathlib were most metaprogramming utilities are grouped into a generalization notion of tactics. While this is justified in the mathematical context, it is much more confusing for the CS context of the stdlib (at least in my view). As such, I think it would be a good idea to rename this folder to something more general (like Meta, Lang, or Elab) and/or split the folder into many (e.g., Tactic, Command, DSL, etc.) as stdlib is likely to have an even larger variety of metaprogramming utilities than mathlib.
