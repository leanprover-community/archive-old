Donald Sebastian Leung (Aug 16 2020 at 01:47):

If the kata was originally in Coq and only later translated to Lean then one can fork the original Lean translation, apply the changes necessary (and confirm that Lean v3.18.4 is selected) and submit the fork for review and approval. Otherwise, if it was originally authored in Lean then only the original author (or a moderator with 6k+ honor) may edit the kata - in that case, feel free to PM me the fixes and I will apply them.
