Evgenia Karunus (Nov 19 2022 at 00:00):

Any idea how I can access the docstring of the calling method in Lean 4 code:

elab "add_tactic_doc " tdeStx:term : command => do
  let doc := -- "My docstring"
  ...

/-- My docstring -/
add_tactic_doc tde

In Lean 3 it was done with interactive.decl_meta_info:

@[user_command] meta def add_tactic_doc_command (mi : interactive.decl_meta_info) (_ : parse $ tk "add_tactic_doc") : parser unit := do
  pe ← parser.pexpr,
  tde ← eval_pexpr tactic_doc_entry pe,
  tactic.add_tactic_doc tde mi.doc_string .
