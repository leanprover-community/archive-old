Gabriel Ebner (Mar 16 2022 at 18:28):

This is also an option, moving the -a' to the other side first.

example (a' a : ℕ) : -↑a' ≤ ↑a :=
  Int.le_of_add_le_add_right (b := a') (by simp; norm_cast; simp [Nat.zero_le])
