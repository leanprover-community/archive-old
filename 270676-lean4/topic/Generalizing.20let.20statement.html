Alexander Bentkamp (Jun 29 2022 at 14:49):

Can you give a concrete example? Do you mean something like this?

example : let x := 0; x + 5 = 5 := by
  intro x
  generalize h : x = x'
  cases x'
  · exact Nat.zero_add 5
  · exact Nat.noConfusion h
