Mario Carneiro (Aug 01 2022 at 13:41):

class is actually an attribute you can put on an inductive or structure, i.e. class is shorthand for @[class] structure and class inductive is shorthand for @[class] inductive

Mario Carneiro (Aug 01 2022 at 13:42):

The @[class] attribute just means that it participates in typeclass inference, which is mostly orthogonal to structure / inductive which is about the data representation and what the elements of the type are
