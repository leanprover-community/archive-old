Gabriel Ebner (Dec 01 2021 at 10:40):

If you're on nixos and leanc segfaults, then this most likely means you need a newer elan version from nixpkgs. Unfortunately this is not visible in the version number, both the segfaulting and non-segfaulting one have version 1.3.1. After upgrading, you also need to remove all the old toolchains (rm -rf ~/.elan also works).
