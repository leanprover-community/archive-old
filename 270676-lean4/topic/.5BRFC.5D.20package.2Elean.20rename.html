Mac (Sep 26 2021 at 04:55):

I am planning on renaming package.lean to something more generic in preparation for generalizing Lake beyond just building package targets. I also want to do this now, while Lake is in its early stages, when such a change is unlikely to cause much pain.

Currently, the two names I have in mind are the basiclakefie.lean and the more thematic, but esoteric watershed.lean. I am also considering using the later name instead for an eventual lockfile for Lake (i.e., watershed.json would instead be the JSON lockfile). I would like to know what name the Lean community would prefer. Alternatively, if anyone has another idea for a name feel free to suggest it!

Christian Pehle (Sep 26 2021 at 15:33):

It should be named after what it is supposed to describe. It could use a metaphor to do so (cargo, crate, ...), but I think lake does not necessarily lend itself well to elaborate metaphors (port, harbour, marina, ...?). Something like workspace.lean would make sense if the result of invoking lake would be a working setup potentially consisting of several packages, which all are build and made available for working in VSCode. Otherwise build.lean and setup.lean would come to mind.
