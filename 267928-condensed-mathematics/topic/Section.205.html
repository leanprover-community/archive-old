Peter Scholze (Apr 05 2022 at 11:43):

@Filippo A. E. Nuccio Yes, W=W1W=W_1W=W1​, that is a typo. Everything in sight is supposed to be considered as condensed R\mathbb RR-vector spaces, in particular the pullback. And the problem is that the map W→RW\to \mathbb RW→R summing all xix_ixi​ is not continuous. Namely, any open subset of W≤1W_{\leq 1}W≤1​ contains the basis vector (0,…,0,1,0,…)(0,\ldots,0,1,0,\ldots)(0,…,0,1,0,…) (with 111 in place iii) for sufficiently large iii.
