Rob Lewis (Dec 28 2019 at 20:50):

The official conference hotel is the Wyndham Pittsburgh University Center. Note that there are a couple other Wyndhams in Pittsburgh, so the University Center part is important! If CMU booked a hotel for you, this is where you're staying.

Rob Lewis (Dec 28 2019 at 20:50):

The Wyndham is about an hour by bus from the airport -- take the 28X to Forbes and Bigelow, and walk to the other side of the giant evil-looking tower. I think you can get a ConnectCard at the airport to pay for the buses with stored value. It's probably worth it if you expect to take the bus more than just to/from the airport. If you pay cash, the bus drivers can't make change for you, which is annoying, so get some one dollar bills at the airport. There are also taxis, Lyft, Uber, etc, which will take 30-60 minutes depending on traffic and cost $40-60.
