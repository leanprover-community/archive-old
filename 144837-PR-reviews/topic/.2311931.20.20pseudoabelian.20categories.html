Joël Riou (Feb 11 2022 at 13:10):

Antoine Chambert-Loir said:

Joël Riou , does the karoubian envelope functor have an universal property that mathlib can check?

In some sense, karoubi is more or less a left adjoint to the inclusion of idempotent complete categories in the category of categories.
More precisely, if C is any category and D is idempotent complete, it seems -- I have to remove a few sorries -- that there is an equivalence of categories between the categories of functors C ⥤ D and karoubi C ⥤ D. (More practically, a functor C ⥤ D extends canonically to karoubi C ⥤ karoubi D, and if D is pseudoabelian, we get a functor karoubi C ⥤ D, and natural transformations between functors in karoubi C ⥤ D identifies to natural transformations in C ⥤ D.)
