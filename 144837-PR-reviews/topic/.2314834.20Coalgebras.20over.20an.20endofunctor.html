Julian Külshammer (Jun 27 2022 at 13:01):

:ping_pong: This PR dualises work of @Joseph Hua introducing the category of coalgebras over an endofunctor (that is equivalent to the category of coalgebras of some cofree comonad that might not exist if the underlying category doesn't have products, but this is not shown in the PR). It then shows that if F is adjoint to G, then the category of F-algebras is equivalent to the category of G-coalgebras, a mathematically straightforward statement, that tidy could do almost by itself if it were faster.
