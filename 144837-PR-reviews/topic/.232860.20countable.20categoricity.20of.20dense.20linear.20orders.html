Jalex Stark (Jun 03 2020 at 22:03):

just to record a mistake I fell into when trying to apply these proofs to the Rado graph:
the proofs are simplified by the fact that equality can be recovered from your cmp function. for instance this is needed in order for partial_isos to be thought of as bijective functions between subsets. I added in this bijectivity as a new property of partial_iso and had to deal with nontrivial new proof obligations. Adding equality as one of the cmp outcomes is easier
