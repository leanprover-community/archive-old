In #12056 I introduce two lemmas on isomorphisms, this one and a similar lemma on the right:

is_iso.of_is_iso_comp_left : ∀ {C : Type u} [_inst_1 : category C] {X Y Z : C} (f : X ⟶ Y) (g : Y ⟶ Z)
[hf : is_iso f] [hfg : is_iso (f ≫ g)], is_iso g

(In a future PR, I plan to add similar results for equivalence of categories instead of isomorphisms.)
