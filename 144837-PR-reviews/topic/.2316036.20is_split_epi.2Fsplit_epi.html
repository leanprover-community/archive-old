Joël Riou (Aug 13 2022 at 05:26):

Following a discussion on another PR https://github.com/leanprover-community/mathlib/pull/15857 in PR #16036, I make a distinction between is_split_epi and split_epi in category theory. Currently, split_epi f is a class which contains the datum of a section of f. It is certainly better to have a structure split_epi f which contains the datum of a section, and a class is_split_epi f which asserts the existence of such a section.
