I think the name change is bad in that it does not indicate the key difference between ordinal minimum and the infimum in a complete lattice: sets of ordinals always achieve their minimum, because they are well founded

Violeta Hernández (Jul 16 2022 at 04:50):

This isn't a name change: we completely ditch ordinal.min in favor of the more general infi. We more generally have the theorem that nonempty sets in a well-ordered conditionally_complete_linear_order contain their infimum, see docs#Inf_mem.
