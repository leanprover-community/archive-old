Topic: #14402 The bicategory of algebras and bimodules

{% raw %}

Oleksandr Manzyuk (May 26 2022 at 22:08):

I've finished the work started by @Scott Morrison and generalised the well-known construction of the bicategory of rings, bimodules, and intertwiners. The generalisation replaces rings with monoids in some monoidal category (with coequalizers and tensor products that preserve colimits), and bimodules and intertwiners with suitably defined bimodule objects and their homomorphisms.
