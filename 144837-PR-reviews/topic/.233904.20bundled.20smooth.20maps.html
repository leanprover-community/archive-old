Those are very different courses. Of course my recommendation is to follow Viterbo in priority. Fejoz and Niederman will be pure dynamical systems, I wouldn't really call that geometry. Since you like Lie groups and physics you'll probably enjoy Ma's course as well. Don't forget to also visit http://symplectic2021.math.cnrs.fr/ especially following the link to the introductory school at https://conferences.cirm-math.fr/2329.html
