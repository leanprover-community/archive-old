The essential mathematics and main theorems are all contained in data/dfinsupp/well_founded.lean. However, a total of 11 files are changed/added in this PR so I spin off two PRs:

#16777 adds two files dfinsupp.ne_locus and dfinsupp.lex which are directly ported from the finsupp counterparts (authored by @Damiano Testa). It also includes the easy PR #16740 which generalizes some linear_order assumptions to partial_order.

#16741 is an easy PR that shows (<) is well-founded in a preorder iff it's well-founded on its antisymmetrization (a partial order). It overlaps with #16720 because both move relation.fibration to another file.
