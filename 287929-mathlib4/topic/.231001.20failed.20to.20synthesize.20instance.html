Kevin Buzzard (Dec 14 2022 at 17:25):

This works:

@[to_additive]
instance {M N} [SMul M N] [SMul M α] [SMul N α] [IsScalarTower M N α] :
    IsScalarTower M N αᵐᵒᵖ :=
  ⟨fun m n (a : α) => unop_injective <| smul_assoc m n a⟩
