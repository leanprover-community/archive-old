Kyle Miller (Jan 17 2023 at 15:11):

Here's a workaround. Change your have to

    have h : TProd.mk l f ∈ Set.TProd l t ↔ ∀ i : ι, i ∈ l → f i ∈ t i := by
      change f ∈ TProd.mk l ⁻¹' Set.TProd l t ↔ f ∈ { x | x ∈ l }.pi t
      rw [mk_preimage_tprod l t]
