Johan Commelin (Jan 13 2023 at 12:42):

I wanted to share a little workflow for updating names (in VSCode). Maybe all you've you know this already. I figured it out yesterday.

  1. select i_am_snake_case

  2. run the following loop:

    • hit Ctrl-D to select the next occurence
    • if the occurence should be skipped, hit Ctrl-K to "kill" that selection
    • (if necessary, hit Ctrl-U to backtrack)

  3. after you have selected all occurences of is_special that need to be changed, make the edit to iAmCamelCase.
